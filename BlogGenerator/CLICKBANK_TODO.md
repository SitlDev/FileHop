### Instructions:
1. Click the 'Find Vendor Here' link to search ClickBank.
2. Once you find a product you like, find its vendor ID (nickname).
3. Create a file named `vendor_mapping.txt` and simply list them:
   `1. Product Title: vendor_id`
   `2. Another Title: second_id`
