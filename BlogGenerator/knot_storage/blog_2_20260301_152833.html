**Orlando City vs. Inter Miami: Preview, Predictions and Lineups**

As the MLS season heats up, two of the league's most exciting teams are set to face off in a highly anticipated matchup. Orlando City SC will take on Inter Miami CF at Exploria Stadium, with both sides looking to make a statement in the standings.

**Orlando City: The Pride Look to Continue Winning Streak**

Orlando City has been on a tear lately, riding a three-game unbeaten streak into this weekend's contest. Led by star striker Tesho Akindele, the Pride have found ways to score crucial goals and shut down opponents' attacks. With a strong mix of veteran players like Chris Mueller and youngsters like Benji Michel, Orlando City has become a force to be reckoned with in the Eastern Conference.

**Inter Miami: The Herons Seek to Rebound from Recent Setbacks**

Despite their promising start to the season, Inter Miami has hit a rough patch lately. After a thrilling 3-2 win over Atlanta United FC, the Herons have dropped two straight matches, including a heartbreaking defeat at the hands of FC Cincinnati. With star midfielder Blaise Matuidi and striker Gonzalo Higuaín leading the charge, Inter Miami knows they need to get back on track if they want to stay in contention for a playoff spot.

**Lineups and Key Matchups**

Orlando City coach Oscar Pareja will likely stick with his usual starting XI, featuring Akindele up front and Mueller providing width on the wings. Inter Miami manager Phil Neville may make some adjustments to his lineup, potentially inserting winger Robbie Robinson or midfielder Victor Ulloa to spark their attack.

**Prediction**

In a tightly contested match, I predict Orlando City will emerge victorious by a score of 2-1. The Pride's strong defense and ability to create scoring chances from midfield should give them the edge they need to take all three points.
