## 💡 PRO-TIPS FOR QUORA SUCCESS:
1.  **Format for Skimmers:** Use **Bold** for the final answer and bullet points for the steps.
2.  **Use Images:** If possible, take a screenshot of your calculator's result and paste it into the Quora answer. Answers with images get 3x more views.
3.  **Be the 'Last Answer':** If an existing answer is 5 years old, write a new, modern one with a link to a "2026 Updated Calculator."
4.  **Disclose your site:** Quora likes transparency. It's okay to say "I built this tool to help..." as long as the answer itself is helpful.
