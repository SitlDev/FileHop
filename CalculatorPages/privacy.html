1. Overview

Welcome to YourCalc. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

All calculations run client-side in your browser. We do not store, transmit, or process your inputs on our servers. What you type into the calculators stays on your device.

3. Automatically Collected Data

We collect basic server logs and analytics data (such as IP addresses, browser types, and referring pages) to monitor website performance and security.

4. Advertising & Cookies

Google AdSense serves ads on this site using cookies. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits to this or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings. Our third-party ad/analytics partners include Google AdSense and Google Analytics.

5. Cookie Policy

We use functional cookies (session only) to maintain site state, and third-party advertising cookies (Google AdSense) to deliver relevant ads. No personally identifiable information is stored in our cookies. Users can disable cookies in their browser settings; however, some site features may be affected.

6. CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)

We do not sell personal information. California residents have the right to know what data is collected (none beyond page view analytics). To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at admin@knotstranded.com.

7. GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)

For EU/EEA residents: we process no personal data directly. Google AdSense may process data per their own GDPR policies; please refer to Google's privacy policy. The legal basis for any incidental processing is legitimate interest.

8. Data Retention

We retain no user-submitted data. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics default settings (26 months).

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at admin@knotstranded.com.
