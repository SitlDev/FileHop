Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

At The Worn Pages Club (a product of KnotStranded LLC), we treat your data with the same reverence we treat a vintage first edition. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you enter our sanctuary.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including your name, email address, shipping address, and the literary preferences you share during your onboarding quiz. We also collect payment information via our secure processors (Stripe/PayPal).

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used primarily to curate your literary experience. We use your taste profile to select books, your address to dispatch your treasures, and your email to send curator dispatches and sanctuary updates.

3. Curation Privacy

Your literary taste profile is personal. We never sell or share your specific reading history or preferences with third parties for marketing purposes. Your journey is yours alone.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use subtle cookies to remember your preferences and analyze how visitors interact with the sanctuary. This helps us refine the experience and ensure our dispatches reach the right readers.

5. Your Rights

You may access, update, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at legal@thewornpages.com.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Contact Us

Questions about our privacy practices? Reach out to us at privacy@thewornpages.com.
