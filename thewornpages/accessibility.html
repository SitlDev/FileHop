Accessibility Statement

The Worn Pages Club is committed to ensuring that our digital sanctuary is accessible to everyone, regardless of ability. We believe that the magic of vintage literature should be shared by all.

Our Commitment

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA standards. Our goal is to provide a seamless experience for users utilizing screen readers, keyboard-only navigation, and other assistive technologies.

Current Initiatives

  • Ensuring high color contrast for all text elements.
  • Providing descriptive Alt-text for all decorative and functional images.
  • Maintaining a clear, semantic HTML structure for easy navigation.
  • Ensuring all buttons and interactive elements are keyboard-accessible.

Ongoing Improvements

While we are proud of our current accessibility standards, we recognize that this is an ongoing journey. We regularly review our sanctuary to identify and remove any barriers to entry.

Feedback

If you encounter any difficulty accessing any part of our site, or if you have suggestions on how we can improve our accessibility, please let us know at accessibility@thewornpages.com.
