❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I toggle dark mode?

Press Ctrl+Shift+D (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+D (Mac), or click the extension icon in your toolbar. You can also right-click any page and select "Toggle Dark Mode".

Will it work on every website?

Yes, on ~99% of websites. Some corporate intranets or heavily secured sites may block style injection, but these are rare.

Does it track my data?

No. All your settings are stored locally on your device. We don't collect, track, or share any personal data.

Can I sync settings across devices?

With the free version, settings are stored per device. Premium users can enable cloud sync to keep settings in sync across all devices logged into the same Google account.

How do I disable dark mode for specific sites?

Click the extension icon and toggle the switch for the current site. You can have different settings for each site.

Is there a free trial for Premium?

The free version is very complete. Premium adds themes, cloud sync, and unlimited CSS rules if you need them. Start with free and upgrade anytime.
