FAQs

How does smart routing work?

We check your country and automatically pick the best print provider: Printful for US/MX, Gelato for EU/UK/AU/CA, and Printify everywhere else.

Can I track my order?

Yes — every order is saved to your profile with a status timeline. When a carrier tracking number is available from the print provider, we link directly to it.

How do I reorder?

Open your profile (top right), find any past order, and click Reorder. It pre-fills your cart with the same design, size, and shipping address in one click.

What file formats can I upload?

PNG (transparent background recommended), JPG, and SVG. For best print quality, use high-resolution files at 300 DPI or higher.

Can I order in bulk?

Yes! 5+ units get 5% off, 10+ get 10% off, 25+ get 15% off, 50+ get 20% off. Contact us for 100+ unit custom pricing.

What products can I print on?

30+ products: tees, hoodies, crop tops, tank tops, hats, beanies, tote bags, mugs, travel tumblers, water bottles, phone cases, laptop sleeves, mouse pads, pillows, blankets, posters, canvas prints, socks, aprons, notebooks, sticker sheets, and more. Open the Product Selector to see all options with pricing.

Which products can't be fulfilled automatically?

Our current catalog only includes products available for automated fulfillment via Printful, Printify, or Gelato. If an item is in our store, it can be shipped worldwide without manual intervention.

How does the 24-hour cancellation window work?

Every order enters a 24-hour buffer before being dispatched to the print provider. You can cancel any order within that window — no fees, no questions. After 24 hours it enters production and can no longer be cancelled.
